How to Test

  1. Open Developer Tools (F12)
  2. Go to the Application tab
  3. Click on Cookies in the left sidebar
  4. Select the current domain
  5. Inspect the cookies and their properties
  6. Try accessing cookies via JavaScript in the Console (HttpOnly cookies won't be accessible)
